# PromptBin Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy is for PromptBin's published web extension. PromptBin is designed to keep your prompt library private and stored on your device. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is used.

## Data PromptBin handles

PromptBin stores the prompts, shortcuts, preferences, and recent-use information you choose to create. This data is stored locally in your browser and is not collected by or sent to the developer.

PromptBin does not collect analytics, browsing history, account details, or personal information. It does not sell or share your data with advertisers or other third parties.

## Supported AI chat websites

PromptBin runs only on the supported AI chat websites listed in its permissions. It reads and updates the chat message box as needed to detect a shortcut and replace it with your saved prompt.

When you send a message, the extension may process that outgoing message locally to perform the replacement. The completed message is sent directly to the AI service you are using, and that service's own privacy policy applies.

## Backups and imports

You can export your prompt library as a file and import it later. Exported files remain under your control, and PromptBin does not upload them anywhere.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data remains in your browser until you delete it or uninstall PromptBin. You can delete individual prompts within the extension, and uninstalling the extension removes its browser-stored data.

## Security

PromptBin limits website access to the supported AI chat services required for its features. No method of local storage is completely risk-free, so avoid saving highly sensitive information in prompts.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if PromptBin's features or data practices change. Any updated version will include a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact provided on PromptBin's extension store listing.
